Robots

Duckieboat

An Unmanned Surface Vehicle(USV), which is an educational platform dor people who want to learn marine robotics or machine learning, and also can do aquatic human rescue, water environment maintenance...etc. missions. My job is Duckieboat design and assemble.

The paper about the whole Duckieboat operating platform(Duckiepond) had selected by IROS and presented at Macao in 2019.

Husky

Husky is an Unmanned Ground Vehicle(UGV), I built it for a contest named Subterranean Challenge holds by DARPA holds by DARPA, which aims to develop innovative technologies that would augment operations underground to execute search and rescue mission. My job is hardware system design and assemble.

Our team(Team NCTU) got seventh place and the fans favorite prize at the first round of Subterranean Challenge at 2018.

More about Husky

Jackal

Jackal is an Unmanned Ground Vehicle(UGV), which is smaller and lighter than husky, aims to perform an efficient search and rescue mission. We build it also for Subterranean Challenge helds by DARPA. My job is hardware system design.

We plan to bring Jackal to the Final round of Subterranean Challenge at 2021.
